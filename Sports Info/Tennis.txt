Tennis is a game played with two opposing players (singles) or pairs of players (doubles) using tautly strung rackets to hit a ball of specified size, weight, and bounce over a net on a rectangular court.
Tennis is a sport played with a felt-covered rubber ball, a tennis racket, and a court. ... Tennis's official name is "lawn tennis". First, early in the 11th century, players in France played a sport like this with their hands. It was called “Jeu de Paume”.
